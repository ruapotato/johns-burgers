John's Burgers

910 W Central Ave, Sutherlin, OR · Mon-Fri 9-5

About

I've been doing this for twenty-five years. The whole family says I make the meanest burger they've ever had — come on by and see for yourself.

Menu

Signature Burgers

The Classic Heavyweight — Double 1/4 lb beef patties, melted American cheese, crisp lettuce, tomato, sliced onions, and "Trap Sauce" on a toasted brioche bun.
Smokehouse BBQ Stack — Single beef patty topped with applewood smoked bacon, sharp cheddar, crispy fried onion straws, and tangy BBQ sauce.
The Midnight Shift — Beef patty, fried egg, hash brown, pepper jack cheese, and spicy aioli for that late-night energy boost.
Mushroom Swiss Melt — Sauteed garlic mushrooms and melted Swiss cheese over a flame-grilled beef patty with truffle mayo.
The Green Glitch (Veggie) — A seasoned plant-based patty with avocado, sprouts, pickled red onions, and chipotle ranch.

Sides & Snacks

Salty Dog Fries — Hand-cut fries tossed in sea salt and cracked black pepper.
Loaded "Trash" Tots — Tater tots smothered in beer cheese, bacon bits, jalapenos, and green onions.
Beer-Battered Rings — Thick-cut onion rings served with a side of horseradish dipping sauce.

Shakes & Drinks

Oil Spill Shake — Rich chocolate milkshake with crushed Oreo cookies and whipped cream.
Neon Citrus Soda — A bright, fizzy lime and orange soda with a caffeine kick.
Bottomless Joe — Black coffee, served in a ceramic mug that's definitely seen better days.
